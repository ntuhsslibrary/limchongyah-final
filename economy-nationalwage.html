Economy - National Wage Council

Professor Lim Chong Yah, also known as the face of Singapore economic, is a prolific researcher who has written extensively on the theory of economic development and the policy options of the Singapore economy. His writing on the Singapore economy in particular has influenced many important changes in Singapore’s economic and social policies, particularly during his days with the National Wages Council (NWC).

National Wage Council Other Topics

In close to three decades, Professor Lim was the Founding Chairman of the National Wages Council (NWC) which contributed much to the economic development of Singapore. During his tenure as the Chairman of the NWC, he contributed to achieve and maintain industrial peace in Singapore, strategized the late 1970s’ restructuring of the Singapore’s economy to one of higher value-added.
